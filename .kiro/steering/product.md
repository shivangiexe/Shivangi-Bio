# Product Overview

This is a personal portfolio website for Shivangi, a student focused on building skills across design, presentations, AI tools, and coding. The site showcases her approach to learning and career development.

## Key Features

- **Personal Branding**: Clean, modern design with animated elements and macOS-inspired UI
- **Skills Showcase**: Interactive skill cards highlighting design, presentations, coding, and AI tools
- **Philosophy Section**: Emphasizes "progress over hype" and consistent learning
- **Social Connection**: Direct link to Instagram profile (@shiivv.exe)

## Design Philosophy

- **No Hype, Just Growth**: Focus on real skills and meaningful progress
- **Consistency Over Perfection**: Small daily improvements over occasional bursts
- **Smart Work**: Leveraging the right tools to build efficiently

## Target Audience

- Potential employers and collaborators
- Fellow students and learners
- Professional network connections

The site reflects a student's journey of intentional skill-building with a focus on practical, career-oriented development.
